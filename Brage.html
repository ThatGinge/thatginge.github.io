Brage Richenberg

About the Pro

Brage Richenberg is a Norwegian snowboarder praised for his natural style and swagger. Whatever he does on the snow, he finds a way to make it look super cool. At 22, Brage is one of the youngest riders out there, and although he is already a great snowboarder, Brage has massive potential to improve and become one of the best.
