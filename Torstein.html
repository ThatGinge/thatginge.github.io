Torstein Horgmo

About the Pro

Torstein Horgmo is a Norwegian professional snowboarder. He often competes in both slopestyle and Big Air competitions, and is most well known for performing the first Triple Cork in a competition. This technically demanding trick earned him gold in that competition, and a spot on the podium in many others.
