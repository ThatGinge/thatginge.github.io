Travis Rice

About the Pro

Travis Rice is a professional mountain snowboarder from America. He was listed as #13 on Snowboarder Magazine's list of most influential snowboarders. Travis is most well known for his huge movie, The Art of Flight. In the movie, Travis and friends travel the world, searching for new experiences in backcountry snowboarding. He prides himself on his "freestyle mountain" riding, where he shreds down mountains never before touched by man.
