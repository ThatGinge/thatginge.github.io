Mark McMorris

About the Pro

Mark McMorris is an olympic medalist snowboarder from Regina, Saskatchewan. At 21, he is one of the youngest pro's in the world right now, and he has proved his worth at many competitions. In the 2014 Sochi Olympics, Mark recieved a bronze medal for slopestyle, despite having fractured his ribs just weeks before.
